## Initial Diagnostic: Phone Use and Reading Habit

|---|---|---|---|
| at daily life | in daily life / in my daily life | preposition/collocation | Use `in` for life context. |
| I faced a problem | I have a problem | naturalness | `faced` is possible, but `have` is more natural for current personal issues. |

| feel sadness | feel sad / feel bad about it | word form/naturalness | Use adjective or natural phrase. |

## Recurring Focus Areas

- Prepositions in common expressions
- Countable vs uncountable nouns: time, advice
- Verb patterns: keep doing, spend time doing, make time for
- Natural phrasing instead of literal translation
- Conversation-ready chunks
